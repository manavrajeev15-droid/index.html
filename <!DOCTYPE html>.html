What We Do

Custom designs tailored to you.

My Trick Pony is a boutique custom apparel and graphics studio in San Francisco's Mission District. We work hands-on with every client — whether you arrive with a fully-formed concept or just a rough idea — and we help carry it all the way through to a finished product you'll be proud to wear.

We specialize in small-run, high-quality production using time-tested techniques like silk-screen and embroidery alongside modern methods like digital heat transfer and airbrushing.

What We Offer

Every technique. One studio.

We offer a comprehensive range of custom decoration services — all produced with careful attention to craft, in our San Francisco studio.

01
🎨

Custom Graphic Design

Our design process starts with your brief and ends with artwork that's truly yours. We develop original illustrations, logos, typography layouts, and full brand identities — all optimized for print production from day one.

Illustration Logo Design Typography Brand Identity
02
👕

T-Shirt & Apparel Printing

From single pieces to large runs, we print on t-shirts, hoodies, tote bags, hats, and more. We source quality blanks and will advise on the best garment and printing combination for your needs and budget.

T-Shirts Hoodies Totes Hats All Garments
03
🖨️

Silk-Screen Printing

The gold standard for bold, vibrant, long-lasting prints. Ideal for runs of 12+ pieces, silk-screen delivers sharp, saturated color and an authentic, tactile quality that digital methods can't replicate.

Multi-Color Spot Color Discharge Specialty Inks
04
🧵

Embroidery

Classic and durable, embroidery adds dimension and prestige to any garment. Perfect for polos, jackets, caps, and uniforms. We handle both flat and 3D puff embroidery with fine detail and color accuracy.

Flat Embroidery 3D Puff Patches Uniforms
05
🔥

Heat Transfer Printing

A versatile option for short runs, full-color photographic images, and specialty finishes. Heat transfer allows for fine detail and gradient reproduction on a wide variety of fabric types and colors.

Full Color Short Runs Vinyl HTV Sublimation
06
🌬️

Airbrushing

For truly one-of-a-kind pieces, our airbrushing service delivers smooth gradients, photo-realistic effects, and hand-crafted artistry that no machine can replicate. Available for individual garments and custom art pieces.

Our Process

How every project gets made.

Step 01

Consultation

We discuss your project in detail — concept, quantities, timeline, and budget. All consultations are by appointment at our Mission District studio.

Step 02

Design & Approval

Our team develops custom artwork based on your brief. We iterate together until you're completely satisfied — no production begins without your sign-off.

Step 03

Production

We select the most suitable print method and execute your order with precision. Most projects are completed within 5–10 business days after approval.

Step 04

Delivery & Pickup

Pick up your order at our studio or arrange local delivery. We quality-check every piece before it leaves our hands.

About Us

Where every idea
gets a fighting chance.

My Trick Pony was born out of a simple belief: that custom work should be truly custom. Not templated, not rushed, and not handed off to a machine the moment you walk out the door.

We're a small, dedicated studio in San Francisco's Mission District, and we've spent years honing our craft across every major print and embroidery technique in the industry. From silk-screening to airbrushing, we've invested in the skills and equipment to do every job properly.

Every project we take on is done by appointment — because we believe good work starts with a real conversation. We want to understand your idea, your audience, and what success looks like for you before a single thread is pulled or a screen is burned.

Whether you're a local band printing your first merch run, a restaurant outfitting its staff, or a designer bringing an original piece to life, we approach every job with the same level of care and attention to detail.
